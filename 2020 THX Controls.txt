Logitech Joysticks: Split Arcade Drive
 
LogitechRB: Switch to Base High Gear(SLOW)
LogitechRightTrigger: Switch to Base Low Gear(FAST)
LogitechLB: Climb Up While Held
LogitechLeftTrigger: Climb Down While Held

XBox A: Start/Stop Flywheel(Do this before shooting)
XBox B: Shoot Ball While Held
XBox X: Intake Toggle Down/Up
XBox Y: Collect Balls While Held (Intake, Storage In)
XBox RB: Eject Balls While Held (Intake, Storage Out)
